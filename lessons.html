Drew Sarette

Drew is a professional violinist and teacher with 10+ years experience working with students of all ages, experience levels, and backgrounds. He holds a master's degree in violin performance and pedagogy from the University of Louisville, and has completed Suzuki teacher training. Besides teaching violin and viola, he performs frequently with the Huntsville Symphony Orchestra, Orchestra Sul Ponticello, and Orchestra Kentucky Bowling Green. He is passionate about showing students how to become their own teacher, and become lifelong musicians!
